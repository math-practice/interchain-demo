Investments

map

We invest in projects that expand the ecosystem by:

Addressing transparency, security and real-world applications for open, decentralized networks.

We invest in projects that expand the ecosystem by addressing transparency, security and real-world applications for open, decentralized networks. We seek to develop strategic niches that expand and increase the capabilities, adoption, and overall value of the Cosmos Network.

Broad areas we invest in include:

  • Privacy, Anonymity, and Cryptography
  • Scalability
  • Virtual Machines
  • Bridges and Peg Zones
  • Social Good
  • Geographical / Local Currencies
  • Growth and Adoption
