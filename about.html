A Vision of Interoperability

The Interchain Foundation funds, stewards, and responsibly advances the Cosmos ecosystem.

The Interchain Foundations stewards a vast ecosystem of decentralized technologies. We develop and maintain foundational protocols, and fund organizations to create an internet of shared resources, security, and value creation.

Through our grant funding, investments, and ecosystem stewardship, we are shaping a new technological paradigm that is open-source, interoperable, community-owned.
